Frequently Asked Questions

What is Vyra?
Vyra(viral) is an Artificial intelligent platform that gives travelers autonomy and real-time metrics on intercity transport services in Nigeria
How does Vyra determine Intercity transport service ratings and data?
Vyra uses a combination of customer reviews and years of intercity transport algorithmic-based data analysis to generate accurate ratings.
Is Vyra free to use?
Yes! Vyra is completely free for users who want to check transport service metrics.
Can I submit my own transport reviews?
Absolutely! You can submit your own experiences and ratings directly on the Vyra platform.
Who can use Vyra?
Any one can use Vyra. Specifically the Travelers.
